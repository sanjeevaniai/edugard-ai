# Model Card — Edu Guard AI

Model Name: Edu Guard AI
Version: 0.9.0
Last Updated: January 2025
Owner: Engineering Team

## Intended Use
AI-powered personalized learning
recommendations for K-12 students.
Recommends courses, learning paths,
and study resources based on
student performance data.

## Training Data
Source: Student performance records
from three school districts
Size: Approximately 180,000 student records
Consent: Covered under school district
data sharing agreements
FERPA compliance: Assumed — not formally verified
COPPA compliance: Under review for
students under 13
